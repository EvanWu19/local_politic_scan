LOCAL POLITICS SCANNER — Quick Start
=============================================

STEP 1: Get your API keys (free)
──────────────────────────────────
A) Anthropic (AI summaries — REQUIRED for plain-English explanations):
   https://console.anthropic.com/  → API Keys → Create Key

B) Congress.gov (federal bills — free, instant):
   https://api.congress.gov/sign-up/  → check your email for the key

C) OpenStates (Maryland state bills — optional, improves MD coverage):
   https://openstates.org/accounts/signup/

STEP 2: Add your keys to .env
──────────────────────────────
Open the .env file in the project root.

Edit these three lines:
   ANTHROPIC_API_KEY=sk-ant-...your-key-here...
   CONGRESS_API_KEY=...your-key-here...
   OPENSTATES_API_KEY=...your-key-here...   (optional)

STEP 3: Run your first full scan
──────────────────────────────────
Open a terminal in this folder and run:

   .venv\Scripts\python main.py scan

This fetches ~100-150 items from:
  • Maryland General Assembly (state bills)
  • Montgomery County Council press releases & hearings
  • MCPS Board of Education news
  • Montgomery County Police press releases
  • 8 local news RSS feeds (WTOP, Maryland Matters, Bethesda Magazine, etc.)
  • Federal bills (once you add your Congress.gov key)

STEP 4: Open your report
──────────────────────────
The HTML report is saved to:
   reports\digest_YYYY-MM-DD.html

Just double-click it to open in your browser.
It includes:
  • Plain-English summaries of each item
  • Relevance score (how much it affects YOU)
  • Politician Tracker (who voted/sponsored what)
  • Color-coded by topic

STEP 5: Daily automatic scans (already scheduled!)
────────────────────────────────────────────────────
The scanner runs every morning at 7:00 AM automatically.
Fresh report will be in the reports\ folder each day.

To check the schedule:
   powershell -Command "Get-ScheduledTask -TaskName 'LocalPoliticsScan'"

To remove the schedule:
   schtasks /delete /tn LocalPoliticsScan /f


USEFUL COMMANDS
───────────────
  Look up a politician:
    .venv\Scripts\python main.py politician "Marc Elrich"
    .venv\Scripts\python main.py politician "Wes Moore"

  Re-generate report without re-scanning:
    .venv\Scripts\python main.py report

  See scan history and database stats:
    .venv\Scripts\python main.py status

  Run a fresh scan right now:
    .venv\Scripts\python main.py scan


WHO ARE MY REPRESENTATIVES?
────────────────────────────
Set USER_CITY, USER_ZIP_CODE, and the *_DISTRICT variables in your .env
so the scanner can prioritize items from your own elected officials.

To look up your districts:
  State legislature:  https://mgaleg.maryland.gov/mgawebsite/Members/Index/senate
  U.S. House:         https://www.house.gov/representatives/find-your-representative
  County Council:     https://www.montgomerycountymd.gov/council/

Tip: Reports automatically flag election-related news. Look for items
     tagged with "election" in red as any election approaches.
