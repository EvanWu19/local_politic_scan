## 2. Why she matters to the listener

CASA does not endorse in every Maryland primary cycle, but when it does, the endorsement moves turnout in the Latino community in Montgomery County, particularly in the Wheaton, Aspen Hill, Long Branch, and parts of the Rockville areas of the county. Frimpong's elevation to a higher-profile services role coincides with a primary cycle in which several candidates on the listener's ballot have CASA-aligned legislative records (Friedson, Jawando, Glass have all co-sponsored the recent county sanctuary-style bills).

CASA's 2026 primary endorsement, if it comes, has not yet been announced as of 2026-05-18. The organization typically endorses within four to six weeks of primary day. Watch for an announcement in late May or early June.
